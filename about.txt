
QuizMe is an online quiz system, a platform to test your knowledge in an interesting and time-saving way. This system will provide an interface to the student to take quizzes on various subjects and topics, also providing the results at the same moment. Another interface is controlled by the administrator or the teacher/instructor. The admin has the right to create quizzes with suitable questions on various subjects, view studentsâ€™ scorecard and remove or disable quiz. The quizzes can be set with or without a timer and negative marking according to the requirement of the administrator. The users as well as the admin have to login with their particulars to enter or create a quiz respectively. QuizMe is an easier approach which competes with the pen and paper mode of taking tests
